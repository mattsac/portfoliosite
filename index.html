Who am I?

I'm a results driven Design Technologist and UX Designer/Developer with an extensive front-end development background. I have a passion for process and believe every experience, web or not, can benefit from a solid design that keeps the user in mind. Having been in roles from UX consultant > graphic designer > back-end and front-end developer, I'm able to bridge the communication gap often found between design and development and keep everone focused on providing an experience that moves the user to their end goal as painlessly as possible.

How do I work?

Where do we start?

What is the end-goal for the business, for the user? How do we determine this? ...by understanding the user! Research, user interviews, stakeholder interviews, personas, all leading to a great user experience that is as simple as possible and lets everyone, business and end user, meet their objectives.

We know what our user needs, where to now?

Information Architecture! How we organize our content has a huge affect on the overall experience. Are things easy to find? Do they make natural sense to the user? Through card sorting exercises, workflow diagrams and taxonomies, we can come to understand how a user expects to interact with our product.

User Interface!

We now know what we want our product to acheive, who it's for, and what the parts are. Now it's time for interaction design. By starting with lo-fi wireframes we can keep the focus on the interactions and test with the user, iterate and test again. Paper and clickable prototypes let us find usability problems before we spend resources on coding down the wrong path.
